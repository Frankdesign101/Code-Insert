Global Certificate in Practical Supply Chain & Sustainable Operations (GC-PSSO)

Duration: 6–8 weeks
Delivery: Live online (recordings available)
Format: Weekly modules + applied assignments + integrated capstone project
Start Date: September 2026

Week 1 – Global Supply Chain Foundations & Systems Thinking

This module provides an end-to-end understanding of supply chains as interconnected ecosystems operating across industries and global markets.

Key topics include:

Understanding supply chain ecosystems and interdependencies

Global trends, disruptions, and enabling technologies

Case studies across sectors (FMCG, healthcare, retail, agribusiness)

✅ Capstone Activity: Select one organisation and define a real supply chain or operations challenge.

Core Reading References

Chopra, S. & Meindl, P. (2019) Supply Chain Management: Strategy, Planning, and Operation (7th ed.). Pearson.

Chapters 1–2: Understanding the Supply Chain

Chapter 3: Supply Chain Performance and Strategic Fit

Christopher, M. (2016) Logistics & Supply Chain Management (5th ed.). Pearson.

Chapters 1–3: Supply Chain Ecosystems and Competitive Advantage

Week 2 – Inventory, Warehousing & Procurement Essentials

A practical introduction to the core building blocks of supply chain execution and control.

Key topics include:

Inventory control systems and stock management

Warehouse planning, layout, and operational performance

Supplier evaluation, sourcing decisions, and negotiation fundamentals

✅ Capstone Activity: Analyse how inventory, warehousing, or sourcing contributes to the chosen challenge.

Core Reading References

Slack, N. & Brandon-Jones, A. (2022) Operations Management (10th ed.). Pearson.

Chapter 13: Inventory Management

Chapter 4: Process Design and Layout (Warehousing Applications)

Chopra, S. & Meindl, P. (2019) Supply Chain Management (7th ed.). Pearson.

Chapter 6: Inventory and Supply Chain Coordination

Chapter 15: Sourcing and Procurement Decisions

Week 3 – Operations Excellence & Lean Fundamentals

Participants develop practical skills in process improvement and operational efficiency.

Key topics include:

Process analysis and Value Stream Mapping

The 7 wastes and operational bottlenecks

Practical continuous improvement tools for workplace application

✅ Capstone Activity: Map one process and identify improvement opportunities.

Core Reading References

Slack, N. & Brandon-Jones, A. (2022) Operations Management (10th ed.). Pearson.

Chapter 15: Lean Operations and Improvement

Reid, R.D. & Sanders, N.R. (2020) Operations Management: An Integrated Approach (7th ed.). Wiley.

Chapters 4–6: Process Strategy and Analysis

Chapter 16: Managing Quality and Continuous Improvement

Week 4 – Data Analytics for Operations & Supply Chain Performance

Building confidence in data-driven decision-making and operational KPIs.

Key topics include:

Excel for operational analysis and demand forecasting

Designing KPI dashboards for supply chain performance

Introduction to Power BI for visual reporting (foundational level)

✅ Capstone Activity: Develop a KPI framework aligned to the capstone challenge.

Core Reading References

Slack, N. & Brandon-Jones, A. (2022) Operations Management (10th ed.). Pearson.

Chapters 10–12: Planning and Control Systems

Chapter 2: Performance Objectives and Measurement

Chopra, S. & Meindl, P. (2019) Supply Chain Management (7th ed.). Pearson.

Chapter 5: Demand Forecasting in Supply Chains

Chapter 4: Supply Chain Drivers and Metrics

Week 5 – Sustainable & Green Supply Chains

Embedding sustainability and ESG into operational and supply chain strategy.

Key topics include:

ESG, circular economy principles, and green logistics

Climate-smart operations and responsible sourcing

Sustainability case studies and practical trade-offs

✅ Capstone Activity: Identify sustainability risks/opportunities and propose realistic interventions.

Core Reading References

Chopra, S. & Meindl, P. (2019) Supply Chain Management (7th ed.). Pearson.

Chapter 10 (edition dependent): Sustainability and Supply Chain Design

Cambridge Institute for Sustainability Leadership (CISL) (2023)
Sustainable Supply Chain and Operations Frameworks. Executive Education Materials.

Applied ESG and circular economy frameworks (introduced during teaching)

Week 6 – Professional Practice & Global Supply Chain Careers

Preparing participants to communicate operational insights and strengthen professional readiness.

Key topics include:

Professional reporting, presentation, and stakeholder communication

CV and LinkedIn optimisation for supply chain careers

Interview preparation and applied career strategy

✅ Capstone Activity: Finalise recommendations and submit the integrated capstone report.

Core Reading References

Slack, N. & Brandon-Jones, A. (2022) Operations Management (10th ed.). Pearson.

Chapter 1: The Strategic Role of Operations

Chapter 18: Operations Improvement and Organisational Learning

Instructor-developed professional reporting templates and executive communication guidance.

Optional Weeks 7–8 – Capstone Completion & Presentation (Extension)

Participants may join an extended enrichment period to present and refine their applied project.

Includes:

Capstone presentations and peer learning

Facilitated feedback and improvement coaching

Final CPD reflection and learning log completion

Integrated Capstone Project (Embedded Across the Programme)

Participants complete one applied project based on:

One organisation or supply chain context

One operational challenge

Weekly assignments that progressively build a professional final report

Final Output: A structured capstone submission (2,500–3,000 words) demonstrating applied competence in supply chain, operations improvement, analytics, and sustainability.
