Programme Overview

The Global Certificate in Practical Supply Chain & Sustainable Operations (GC-PSSO) is a professional development programme designed to build practical, workplace-ready capability in supply chain management, operations excellence, and sustainability.

Delivered through live online sessions, the programme combines expert-led teaching, applied assignments, and a capstone project focused on real operational or supply chain challenges. The emphasis is on practical application, enabling participants to translate learning directly into improved performance within their organisations or professional contexts.

Who This Programme Is For

Working professionals in supply chain, logistics, procurement, and operations

NGO and public-sector staff involved in supply chain and operations delivery

Early-career professionals and graduates seeking applied, employable skills

Professionals interested in sustainable and responsible supply chain practices

Corporate teams requiring structured capability development

What You Will Learn

By the end of the programme, participants will be able to:

Analyse end-to-end supply chain processes and identify performance gaps

Apply operations improvement and Lean principles to practical scenarios

Use data and KPIs to support operational and managerial decision-making

Integrate sustainability and ESG considerations into supply chain activities

Communicate operational insights clearly and professionally to stakeholders

Programme Structure

The programme is delivered over 6–8 weeks and includes:

Live online teaching sessions (typically two sessions per week)

Interactive discussions and case-based learning

Structured weekly applied assignments

A final applied capstone project addressing a real operational or supply chain issue

Session recordings are provided for participants who are unable to attend live.

Assessment & Capstone Project

Learning is assessed through:

Applied individual or group assignments

Participation in discussions and activities

A final capstone project demonstrating practical understanding, analytical thinking, and professional judgement

The capstone project requires participants to analyse a real or simulated supply chain or operations challenge and propose practical, sustainability-informed solutions.

Certification

Participants who successfully complete the programme will be awarded a Certificate of Completion.
The programme is designed for CPD accreditation, and certificates will be issued as CPD-accredited once final approval is confirmed (planned / in progress).
