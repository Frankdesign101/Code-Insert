Programme Content & Structure
Global Certificate in Practical Supply Chain & Sustainable Operations (GC-PSSO)

Duration: 6–8 weeks
Delivery: Live online (with recordings)
Assessment: Embedded applied assignments + integrated capstone project
Certification: CPD-accredited (planned / in progress)

Programme Design Philosophy

The programme is structured around five integrated modules, delivered sequentially over 6–8 weeks.
Each week combines live teaching, guided reading from established operations and supply chain textbooks, and a focused applied assignment.

Weekly assignments progressively build one coherent capstone project based on a single organisation and a single operational or supply chain challenge.

Module 1 (Week 1): Supply Chain Foundations & Systems Thinking
Focus

Understanding the supply chain as an end-to-end system and clearly defining an operational or supply chain problem.

Key Topics

End-to-end supply chain structure (plan–source–make–deliver–return)

Supply chain objectives and trade-offs (cost, service, resilience, sustainability)

Systems thinking in supply chains

Capstone Integration (Week 1 Assignment)

Selection of one organisation or supply chain context

Definition of a clear supply chain or operations challenge

➡️ Forms Section 1 of the final capstone report.
